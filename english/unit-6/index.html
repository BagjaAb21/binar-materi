Kegiatan Belajar 1

Isi bagian yang kosong dengan jawaban yang tepat! Contoh: Today is Monday. Tomorrow is Tuesday. (Hari ini hari Senin. Besok hari Selasa.)
  1. Today is Wednesday. Tomorrow is ______.
  2. Today is Friday. Yesterday was ______.
  3. Today is ______. Tomorrow is Sunday.
  4. Today is ______. Yesterday was Tuesday.
  5. Yesterday was ______. Today is Monday.

Kegiatan Belajar 2

Pilih kata yang tepat untuk melengkapi bagian yang kosong! Contoh: I A. eat breakfast in the morning. (A. eat / B. play). (Saya A. makan sarapan di pagi hari.)
  1. We ______ TV on Sunday. (A. watch / b. write)
    (Kami ______ TV pada hari Minggu.)
  2. They ______ soccer on Saturday. (A. play / B. drink)
    (Mereka ______ sepak bola pada hari Sabtu.)
  3. She ______ water after singing. (A. drink / B. read)
    (Dia ______ air setelah bernyanyi.)
  4. I ______ my hands before eating lunch. (A. wash / B. draw)
    (Saya ______ tangan saya sebelum makan siang.)
  5. He ______ a storybook in the evening. (A. read / B. sing)
    (Dia ______ sebuah buku cerita pada malam hari.)

Kegiatan Belajar 3

Lengkapi bagian yang kosong dengan kata ‘like’ yang berarti ‘suka’ atau ‘dislike’ yang berarti ‘tidak suka’!
  1. I ______ apple. That is my favorite fruit.
    (Saya ______ apel. Itu adalah buah kesukaan saya.)
  2. He ______ carrots. He never eats it.
    (Dia ______ wortel. Dia tidak pernah memakannya.)
  3. My sister ______ reading books. She reads every night.
    (Saudara perempuan saya ______ membaca buku. Dia membaca setiap malam.)
  4. They ______ playing football. That is their favorite sport.
    (Mereka ______ bermain sepak bola. Itu adalah olahraga favorit mereka.)
  5. She ______ swimming. She swims every Saturday.
    (Dia ______ berenang. Dia berenang setiap Sabtu.)
